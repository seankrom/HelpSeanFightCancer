<h1>Fighting Cancer</h1>
<p>Hi, my name is Sean and I am the proud founder of www.helpseanfightcancer.com. My story is my own, but like many of you reading this it started when a loved one was diagnosed with cancer. <strong>The purpose of my site is to bring awareness and HOPE to those who have experienced the heartache and pain that cancer causes.</strong></p>
<p><strong></strong>My mother <a href="http://www.gilmanandvalade.com/2009/marilyn-t-krom/" target="_blank">Marilyn Krom</a> was diagnosed with Uterine Cancer in early 2008.  She was shocked at the diagnosis, and could not believe this was happening  <a href="http://www.helpseanfightcancer.com/about-me/">read more&#8230;</a></p>
<h1>There is HOPE</h1>
<p>Cancer can cause great heartache and it is my hope to help in any way that I can. Don&#8217;t hesitate to <a href="http://www.helpseanfightcancer.com/contact-me/">contact me</a> for anything.</p>
<h1>Uterine Cancer</h1>
<p>In the United States, approximately 42,000 women each year are diagnosed with uterine cancer &#8211; the most common malignancy of the female genital tract and the fourth most common cancer in women.</p>
<p>Uterine cancer forms in tissues of the uterus, which is a pear-shaped organ in the pelvis where a fetus grows. The cervix is at the lower, narrow end of the uterus, and leads to the vagina. Uterine cancer can appear in cells lining the uterus (endometrium) and in muscle or other tissues in  <a href="http://www.helpseanfightcancer.com/uterine-cancer/">read more&#8230;</a></p>
